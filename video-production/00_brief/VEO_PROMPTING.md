# Veo prompting — stop burning credits on screensavers

## Rule

**Abstract prompts = garbage** (blue blobs, particles, void). Jury wants either:
1. **Photoreal B-roll** (stadium, hands + phone, crowd bokeh) — max **1–2 clips** cold open
2. **scrcpy** for everything product — that's the real demo

## Good prompt shape (9:16)

- Camera move named (crane, handheld gimbal, slow push-in)
- Real location (stadium night, dark room, street)
- Lighting concrete (floodlights, sodium, screen glow)
- `photorealistic`, `documentary`, `broadcast`
- Explicit negatives: bubbles, screensaver, abstract CGI

## Credit discipline

- **1 generation** per beat until owner approves preview
- Use `*_silent_9x16.mp4` only in CapCut
- Architecture section (4:45): static diagram or scrcpy — skip extra Veo unless needed
